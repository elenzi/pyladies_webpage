Who we are

We are an international mentorship group with a focus on helping more women become active participants and leaders in the Python open-source community.

Our mission is to promote, educate and advance a diverse Python community through outreach, education, conferences, events and social gatherings. PyLadies also aims to provide a friendly support network for women and a bridge to the larger Python world.

Anyone with an interest in Python is encouraged to participate!

Pyladies impact

Since 2014, an army of 1160 volunteers in the Pyladies community organized 517 events. We've been to 342 cities in 79 countries (see them all on a map).

A total of 11520 incredible women attended events organized by members of our community.

Get involved!

Is there no Pyladies event in your city? You can help make it happen!

We open sourced the Organizer Manual that provides you with all information required to organize Pyladies workshop.

By volunteering to organize, you're joining a vibrant and supportive community of 1160 organizers all over the world!
